Splittr - Arcade Survival Game

Splittr is a keyboard-controlled arcade game. Triangles fall from the top of the screen toward your box at the bottom. When a triangle hits your box, it splits into two smaller boxes, each controlled by a different key pair. Dodge the triangles by moving left and right. The game ends when all your boxes are destroyed. Press any key to start. During gameplay, each box shows its control keys (e.g., D/F to move left/right). Press A on the title screen to view achievements. Press Tab to view the leaderboard.
Your browser does not support the canvas element. Splittr requires an HTML5 canvas to run.
